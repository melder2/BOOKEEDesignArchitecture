Scenarios

Appointment Scheduler
Home Features

Description:

As a patient, I want to be able to schedule an appointment.

As a Bookee user, I want to be able to add service providers to my homepage for quick access to their schedule.

As a Bookee user, I want to be able to search for new service providers, so I am aware of what’s options are available.

Constraints:

Addition and removal of listed subscribers dependent on a variety of possible factors.

Accreditation or certificates needed for providers listed.

Comments:

Based on John, Walter, and Sherry’s scenarios
