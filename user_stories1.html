Sherry- User Story 1

As a healthcare provider, I need a way for patients to schedule an appointment when they need me most.

As a patient, I need to be able to schedule follow-up appointments on my own time.

As the single mom of a child with ADHD, sometimes emergencies come up and I need to cancel appointments at the last-minute.

As a patient with agoraphobia, I need a way to avoid cancellation fees when my disorder won’t allow me to leave my home.

As a healthcare provider, I need a way to quickly reference past notes before my next appointment.

As someone who considers themselves not tech-savvy but also manages their own healthcare practice, I need a way to stay organized with all of my appointments while also being simple enough to use.

As a PRN employee, my schedule changes day-to-day and I need a way to reschedule previous appointments.

As the owner of a growing practice, I need a way to view all new patients for my upcoming day.


Sherry- User Story 2

As a recent graduate, I need a way to organize all of my upcoming appointments.

As a psychologist-in-training, I need a way to quickly view a patient’s relevant notes before their appointment.

As a healthcare provider, I need a way to color-coordinate patients for the upcoming day.

As a healthcare provider, I need a way for my session notes to be kept secure and HIPAA-compliant.

As a new patient, I need a way for my doctor to know what time I’ll be there and any relevant notes.

As a secretary for a doctor’s office, I need a way to quickly summarize our doctors’ workload.

As an overworked doctor, I need a way to organize my appointments so that I can catch my breath between patients.

As a traveling doctor, I need a way for patients to view my availability while I’m on the road.


Sherry- User Story 3

As the head of a major hospital, I need a way for my doctors to quickly overview their busy days.

As a shy patient, I need a simple way to schedule appointments without speaking face-to-face.

As the office coordinator for a busy doctor’s office, I need a user-friendly way to view our availability months in advance.

As someone who is prone to losing their planner, I need a way to store my appointments digitally.

As an “old school” doctor that doesn’t like technology, I need a streamlined menu for my appointment planner.

As a busy patient, I need a way to make sure my doctor is available at the times I need them.

As an older patient, sometimes technology confuses me – which is why I need a no-fuss way to book appointments.

As a patient with Alzheimer's, I need a way for someone to remind me of upcoming doctor’s visits.
